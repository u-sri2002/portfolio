Courier Service Management System for Production Company

December 05, 2023 by Get the full project from GitHub.

Overview

• A production company owns many branches in the local market. So they have several branches in several locations. Each branch is creating the same products and all the product have each and every branch of the company

• When a customer make an order for their products, then the branches of the company deliver the order of their products through their courier service.

• Each and every branch owns a courier service for the shipping purpose. So for this courier service every branch has couriers and vehicles. Every shipment delivers by a vehicle owns to the branch.

• Sometimes a shipment can have some an issue like (customer is not available in the delivery time), then courier can’t the deliver the products, so it will be reported.
